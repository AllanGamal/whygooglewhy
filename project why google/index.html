How to tie a tie?

Oriental Knot (simple know), how to tie a tie step-by-step


  1. Place the backside facing away from you, with the longer and wider end on you left. Let the small end rest right above your belly-button.

  2. Let the wide end go under the small end to the left.

  3. Place the wide end back to the left, over the small end.

  4. Bring the wide end up through the neck loop.

  5. Pull the wide end back down through the loop you just made.

Why do cats purr?

Why do cats purr, and how do they do it?

They purr by pulsing their muscles in their larynx and diaphragm, resulting in a vibrating pattern while inhaling and exhaling, and they purr because they are happy, right? Cats do not only purr when they are happy, they also purr when they are in in stress or in pain. Purring could be a way of communicate and express different feelings, but it could also have a purpose of comforting itself.

And some studies suggest that the frequency of the purr actually could help rebuild bone density, acting like a natural healing mechanism.

Why do we sleep?

We do know that it is essential to life since long enough sleep deprivation can cause death. While sleeping the body does a lot of cellular repairs, and sleep deprivation seems to be very correlated to cardiovascular stress.
